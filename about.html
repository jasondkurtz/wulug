    What is WuLUG all about?

    The Wichita State Linux Users’ Group at Wichita State University is a student org that promotes cybersecurity and free/open-source software. Name aside, we’re a club for anyone into Linux, security, and tech overall.

    Given the nature of the LUG, our membership mostly consists of cybersecurity students, software developers, and tech-interested students from Wichita State's School of Engineering. But we don’t turn anyone away — there are no dues, and you don’t have to be a student to join.

    We usually meet inside Beggs Hall in Room 231 for group events like workshops, and we also collaborate there with other student organizations like WiCyS and IEEE.

    For further details on WuLUG conduct, please consult our bylaws located here.
